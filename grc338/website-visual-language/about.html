Welcome to Carmel Cookie Company, where every bite is made with love, passion, and a sprinkle of sweetness! Founded in 2015 by a local mother-daughter duo, we set out to create the kind of cookies that feel like home—warm, comforting, and full of flavor.

At Carmel Cookie Company, we believe that baking is more than just a craft—it’s a way to bring people together. We’ve spent years perfecting our recipes, using only the finest ingredients to create a variety of delicious cookies, from classic favorites like chocolate chip to unique creations inspired by our love of baking and family traditions.

Whether you’re stopping by for a quick treat or picking up a batch to share, we’re committed to making each cookie with the utmost care and attention to detail. Our cozy shop is a place where you can savor the simple joy of a freshly baked cookie, paired with a warm drink, and maybe even a bit of good conversation.

Thank you for supporting our dream. We’re so glad you’re here, and we hope you enjoy every crumb!

With love, the Carmel Cookie Company Team
